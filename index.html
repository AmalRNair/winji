About Winji Creations

Winji Creations is an emerging force in the Malayalam film industry, dedicated to delivering compelling and original cinematic experiences. As a production house, we believe in the power of storytelling to connect with audiences across generations and cultures.

At Winji Creations, our vision is to contribute to the growth of Malayalam cinema by producing films that resonate with audiences while celebrating the rich cultural heritage of Kerala.

Join us on our journey as we embark on creating meaningful cinema that inspires, entertains, and leaves a lasting impact.
